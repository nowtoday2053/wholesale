Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about selling your house to us

Is it possible to sell a home that is occupied?

Yes, selling a home that is either owner-occupied or rented out is possible. Sometimes we can find creative solutions to allow you to stay in the house until your new home is ready.

Do you offer fair prices for properties purchased?

Yes, we do. Our policy is to purchase homes in their current condition, with cash and without the homeowner having to repair anything. Additionally, we pay the closing costs, there are no agent fees or hidden expenses involved in the transaction.

How long does it take to sell my house?

The timeline for selling your house is flexible and depends on your needs. We can close as fast or slow as you need. It's all up to you. We move on your timeline.

What kind of houses does New Beginnings Properties buy?

We purchase all kinds of real estate. We help individuals and strive to understand and accommodate your unique situation. We offer tailored solutions and have a track record of successfully handling any situation presented to us.

Are there any fees in working with you?

When you sell your house to us, there are no commission fees. Additionally, we usually cover all closing costs.

How do you determine the price to offer on my house?

To determine the offer price for your house, we consider various factors such as its location, condition, and layout. We also evaluate comparable sales in your neighborhood to arrive at a fair value.
